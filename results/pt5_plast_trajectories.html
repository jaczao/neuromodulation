BWhat the curves say

DOwn vs curr, cleanly (disjoint projection)

On the learned projection, own-vs-curr is a cross-study pairing (curr from pt5_iter3, own from pt5_plast_init). The one within-study own-vs-curr for plasticity lives on the disjoint projection (the pt5_er_task_id study): same run, flag flipped. Class-IL, SGD, per-neuron.

Gating each replayed sample by its own task mask P[j] instead of the current task's P[t] lifts final accuracy 0.4483 → 0.4833 (+0.035) and cuts forgetting 0.458 → 0.348 (−0.110). The correction helps most exactly where the wrong-task mask was scrambling replayed samples — but the cell is still far below replay's reach: a rescue of the ablation, not a win.
